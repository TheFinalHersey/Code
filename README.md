### About

This will simulate some physical phenomena or maybe be a tool to simulate a genre of them. Such as the convection inside a star or the electrons fluid dynamics in it, Spacetime warp effects of a massive gravity object, fluid dynamics, atoms or even cellular simulations.

The aim is to optimise and learn. The learning will come in many aspects in optimising and seeing artifacts emerge from the simulation. Maybe also cheat simulation using statistical gradients of sorts.
